DESOLATION

An action RPG game for PC

Enbark on an epic adventure to save Rune-Midgard

Trailer coming soon!

Desolation is an action RPG made with RPG Maker VX Ace.
It is primarily a fan game of Diablo and Ragnarok Online. The game features:

  • A dynamic action combat system.
  • A variety of character classes with unique abilities.
  • A large world with a warp system for faster travel.
  • Tons of dungeons and challenging boss fights.
  • An exciting story told through distinct chapters.
  • 14+ hours of playtime.
Meet new companions with unique abilities, battle your way through dark dungeons, enchanted forests, and deadly deserts. Discover iconic locations such as the floating city of Yuno, the Tower of Geffen, the Cursed Abbey, and many more.

Key Features:

Playable Classes

Alchemist

The alchemist's unyielding dedication to research and experimentation have raised them to the summit of scientific knowledge. They use their proficiency with potions and chemicals to assist them in combat.

Assassin

The assassins inhabit a hidden community within the deep desert. They are skilled in deadly poisons, shadow disciplines, and blindingly fast combat.

Gunslinger

Bored with only target practice in smoggy Einbroch, one particular Gunslinger has decided to take up adventuring. Gunslingers are flashy warriors that are effective in both ranged and melee combat.

Knight

Knights are geared towards sword techniques and are trained to endure large amounts of damage. They serve the king of Midgard and are the first line of defense for the kingdom.

Mage

The Mages study the arcane arts at the Mages Guild. They can dispatch enemies from afar with their elemental spells but are vulnerable in close quarters.

Monk

The monks have walked the path of Acolyte and invested the time and effort to sharpen their body rather than their minds. They are martial artists who use a combination of holy magic and their fists to combat their enemies.

Paladin

A Paladin's faith is his weapon and his shield. They combine martial prowess with powerful enchantments to send monsters back to the abyss.

Priest

With the ability to relate to the spirit, they can conjure powerful holy magic from afar that will hurt their enemies and aid their friends.
